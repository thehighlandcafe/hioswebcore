Need help with our internet? Look below.

  1. First, open the Settings app from your launcher.
  2. Then, click on WiFi or Network and Internet**.
  3. Click on the WiFi network called The Highland Cafe™️ WiFi and follow steps to create/sign into your HiAccount details to connect!

*WiFi-enabled device required.

**WiFi settings' location depends on brand of phone you use.
