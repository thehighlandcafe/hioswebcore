Harmony Social Help

Unsure on how use the features in the 'More' section? Take a look below.

Joining Harmony Social

  1. First, tap the more_vert icon on the top bar of the main Harmony screen.

  2. Tap on the groups Harmony Social option.

  3. Tap the button that says 'Accept Invite' - this will either direct you to the Discord app to accept the invitation.

  4. Once invited, you can use the service as you please.

Launching Harmony Social

    Either:

  1. Launch Discord from your homescreen and navigate to the Harmony Social page,

    2. or,


  2. Tap the more_vert icon on the top bar of the main Harmony screen.

  3. Tap on the Launch Harmony Social button at the bottom of the page.
