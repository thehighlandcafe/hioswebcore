Unsure on how to update HiOSMobile? Take a look below.

Updating HiOSMobile

  1. First, tap the more_vert icon on the top bar of the main HiOSMobile screen.
  2. Tap the settings Settings option.
  3. Then tap the system_updateUpdates option.
  4. Then tap the system_updateManually Update HiOSMobile option.
  5. Then, the latest version of HiOSMobile will download as a .apk file.
  6. Once downloaded, tap on the file name in your notifications. Google Play will prompt you to scan the app, tap Scan, and then Update.
