Unsure on how to do something with this app for your stay? Take a look below.

Booking a Room

  1. First, tap the hotel icon on the bottom navigation bar.
  2. Tap 'Book'.
  3. Fill in the form to book a room at weB&B.

Checking-In

  1. First, tap the hotel icon on the bottom navigation bar.
  2. Tap 'Arriving'.
  3. Fill in the form to check-in to weB&B.

Checking-Out

  1. First, tap the hotel icon on the bottom navigation bar.
  2. Tap 'Leaving'.
  3. Fill in the form to check-out to weB&B.
