Hotel Help

Unsure on how to do something with this app for your stay? Take a look below.

Booking a Room

  1. First, tap the hotel icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Tap on whichever hotel you are staying at, e.g. 'WorstEastern'.

  3. Tap 'Book a Room'.

  4. Fill in the form to book a room at the hotel you want to stay at.

Checking-In

  1. First, tap the hotel icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Tap on whichever hotel you are staying at, e.g. 'WorstEastern'.

  3. Tap 'Check-In'.

  4. Fill in the form to check-in to the hotel you want to stay at.

Checking-Out

  1. First, tap the hotel icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Tap on whichever hotel you are staying at, e.g. 'WorstEastern'.

  3. Tap 'Check-Out'.

  4. Fill in the form to check-out of the hotel you want to stay at.
